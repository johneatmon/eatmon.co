## Apps I use

**Coding**

- VS Code with the [Vesper++](https://marketplace.visualstudio.com/items?itemName=Obstinate.vesper-pp) theme

## Coffee

- [Fellow Ode Brew Grinder (Gen 1)](https://fellowproducts.com/products/ode-brew-grinder): A good coffee grinder is one of the best ways to level up your coffee game; the Ode is a great choice for most people
- [Hario V60 Drip Scale](https://www.hario-usa.com/collections/scales/products/v60-drip-scale): A decent scale for the price, but I will probably replace it soon because it's not as precise as I'd like
- [Chemex (8 cup)](https://www.amazon.com/Chemex-Classic-Pour-over-Glass-Coffeemaker/dp/B000I1WP7W): Probably my favorite way to brew coffee at the moment
- [Bodum French Press (34 oz.)](https://www.amazon.com/Bodum-Chambord-French-Coffee-Chrome/dp/B01N9HZJ8R): A classic way to brew coffee; I alternate between brewing with the Chemex and this
- [Fellow Atmos Vacuum Canister](https://fellowproducts.com/products/atmos-vacuum-canister): Keeps your coffee fresh longer
- [Bonavita Gooseneck Kettle (1.7L)](https://bonavita.co/products/1-0l-variable-temperature-kettle): A gooseneck kettle helps you control the flow of water when brewing coffee
